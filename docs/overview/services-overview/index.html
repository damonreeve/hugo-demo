Services Summary

Ozone provides the following advertising services:

Customer Service Description
Publishers Bid Request Transmission Server-to-server transmission of bid requests to Ad Partners under a publisher > Ad Partner Agreement
Managed Demand Monetisation of Publisher inventory and data through Ozone Audience to Advertisers & Ad Partners
Curated Demand Monetisation of Publisher inventory to Ad Partners under an Ozone > Ad Partner Agreement
Insights and Analytics Dashboards, Insights reports and log-level data
Advertisers and Agencies Ozone Audience Managed Service Managed delivery of audience targeting campaigns
Ozone Audience Deals Audience targeting made available through self-serve deals
Ozone Audience Buyer Console Coming Soon
Planning and Insights Audience Planning API (coming soon), Campaign Dashboards, Insights reports and log-level data
Data Room Compliant merging of audience data for custom audience targeting and insights
Ad Partners Bid Request Transmission Server-to-server transmission of bid requests to Ad Partners under a publisher > Ad Partner Agreement
Curated Supply Monetisation of Publisher inventory to Ad Partners under an Ozone > Ad Partner Agreement
